# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability in this project, please report it responsibly.

**How to reach us:** Find the maintainer's contact information from their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/spkerber) or [LinkedIn profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/spencerkerber) and get in touch directly.

We appreciate your efforts to disclose your findings in a responsible manner.
