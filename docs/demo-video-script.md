## Example use cases (for script and voiceover)

**Primary demo flow:** Use one concrete question for the live walkthrough (e.g. restaurant line or venue check).

**Optional framing:** Mention other angles in a title card or voiceover so judges see the range.

### Local / everyday

- **Restaurant line** – "Hot restaurant, no reservations — is there a line outside right now?" Requesters get verified answer + photo evidence instead of calling or guessing.

### Events / news

- **Protest / presence** – "Are riot police at the protest?" or "Is the crowd still at X location?" Verification for events and safety/awareness.

### Prediction and betting markets

- **Kalshi / Polymarket** – Verification as the **proof point** for resolving a market: "Did X happen?" Photo + attested answer = evidence for winner/loser. Same need as **community notes** — verifying that something exists or happened in the real world.

### Script framing line

> "Sabi delivers verifiable, photo-backed answers that can serve as proof for markets, events, or local decisions — like a community note, but on demand and paid."
